the story for Caramel

I decided to open Caramel Cafe one sunny morning while sipping on a rich caramel latte. As the warm, velvety notes of caramel enveloped my senses, a simple thought crossed my mind - why not share this delightful experience with the world? The idea of creating a cozy space where people could escape from their busy lives and savor the simple pleasure of caramel-flavored delights inspired me to turn that dream into reality. And so, Caramel Cafe was born, a place where sweetness and comfort come together to create unforgettable moments.
